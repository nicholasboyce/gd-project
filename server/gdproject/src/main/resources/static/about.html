About

The GLA was established in 2012 and is the result of the Government of Grenadaâ€™s Public Sector Modernization Program. Its primary purpose is to ensure that the land resource is used in an efficient and responsible way to achieve the sustainable development of the nation. By merging the various land functions under one Agency and using such technologies as eGovernment and computer mapping the GLA will be able to deliver public service more efficiently. For example, one of the initiatives (branded as eLandGrenada) will provide citizens and investors with 24/7 access to land related information.
